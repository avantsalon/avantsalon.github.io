Our Story

Our Story

Chong Pak has owned Avant Salon since 2002. She has been in the beauty industry for over 30 years and is a renown cosmetologist in the Midwest.

Chong's daughter, Bella Kim, is also a stylist and the salon manager.

When combining Chong's master cutting and coloring skills with Bella's trendy vision and creativity, Avant caters exquisite salon services. Not only does Avant provide immaculate services, but we only carry salon exclusive products to ensure our clients receive the best of the best.
